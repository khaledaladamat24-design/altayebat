# SEO Strategy

## In scope

- Public marketplace pages on the web app
- Category, offer, vendor, product, and search/discovery routes
- Public policy/legal content such as `/privacy-policy`

## Out of scope

- Authenticated or operational app surfaces that are not intended to rank
- `/admin`
- `/vendor-dashboard`
- `/wallet`
- `/payment-methods`
- `/orders` and `/orders/:id`
- `/account`
- `/checkout`
- `/cart`
- `/auth`, `/register`, `/settings`, `/splash`

## Target audience

- Shoppers in Jordan looking for healthy food, keto products, grocery items, and local vendor delivery
- Arabic-first users, with English as a secondary language

## Primary keywords

- Healthy food delivery Jordan
- Keto food Jordan
- Grocery delivery Jordan
- Online healthy grocery Jordan
- Unknown beyond the product/category catalog — refine later with keyword research
